SDG-Aligned Research Outputs by UNSW Faculties (2017–2024)

This network diagram visualises the connections between UNSW faculties and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), based on journal article output from 2017 to 2024. It highlights the top 40 Faculty–SDG pairings by number of journal articles published, as indexed in the Dimensions database. SDG alignment is based on automated classifications provided by Dimensions. The thickness of each connecting line reflects the volume of SDG-aligned publications, offering insight into the areas where UNSW faculties are most active in contributing to global sustainability goals.
